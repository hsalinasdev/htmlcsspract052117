Scramled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favourite foods. her is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs

Ingredients

    2 eggs 1tbs butter 2tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt buter in a Frying pan over medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and crean in a bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a patula fold the eggs from the ege of the pan to the center every 20 seconds (as if you are making an ommellete)
  5. When eggs are still mois remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served)
